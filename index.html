Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bulk SMS?

Bulk SMS is a service that allows businesses and organizations to send large volumes of text messages to many recipients at the same time. It is commonly used for notifications, promotions, reminders, and alerts.

How fast are messages delivered?

Messages are usually delivered within seconds. Delivery speed may vary slightly depending on the recipient's mobile network and location.

Can I use my company name as the Sender ID?

Yes, you can use your company name as the Sender ID. It must be approved and should not exceed 11 characters, including spaces.

Do I need technical skills to use the platform?

No. Our Bulk SMS platform is designed to be simple and user-friendly. You can upload contacts, compose your message, and send SMS in just a few steps.

Do unused SMS units expire?

No. Unused SMS units remain in your account and can be used whenever you need them.

Can I schedule messages to be sent later?

Yes. You can schedule SMS messages to be sent at a specific date and time, allowing you to plan campaigns and reminders in advance.
